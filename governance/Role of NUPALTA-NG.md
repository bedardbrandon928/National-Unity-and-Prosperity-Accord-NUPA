# NUPALTA-NG

**NUPALTA-NG** (the National Unity and Prosperity Land Trust Agency – Nigeria) is the **private-sector facilitator agency** responsible for the initial implementation and "bootstrapping" of the NUPA-NG framework. It is designed as a "lean" entity that operates at **zero taxpayer cost**, with its primary goal being to establish independent local governance and then **dissolve (sunset)** once those boards are operational.

Its core responsibilities include:

### **1. Hub Activation and Site Verification**
NUPALTA-NG acts as the "scout" for the framework, identifying and surveying idle state-managed land to be pulled out of dormant status. Its duties in this phase include:
*   **Geospatial Mapping:** Conducting technical mapping and site verification of 4,000-hectare hub parcels.
*   **Site Scouts:** Locating prime industrial hectares near existing transmission lines, gas pipelines, and fiber backbones.
*   **Option Agreements:** Coordinating with corporate partners to secure "Option Agreements" for specific parcels once a State Governor executes a Memorandum of Communal Understanding (MOCU).

### **2. Governance and Board Chartering**
One of its most critical roles is the establishment of the **VQPV-NG** (Variable Quad-Partite Vetting) governance boards. Responsibilities include:
*   **Board Seating:** Chartering and seating the four-person local boards, which consist of a State Representative, a Fiduciary Expert, a Local Community Official, and a Seed Beneficiary.
*   **CLT Formation:** Establishing the initial **Community Land Trusts (CLTs)** and locating the first "seed beneficiaries" to lead them.

### **3. Corporate Onboarding and Fiduciary Management**
NUPALTA-NG manages the transition of private capital into the hubs during the "Ignition" phase.
*   **"Spark Group" Management:** Facilitating the reservation deposits (typically ₦40 million per 100-hectare block) from Tier-1 global and indigenous firms.
*   **Verification Services:** Running forensic lineage verification—often called the **Verification Assistance Program**—to identify the "Original Stewards" and verified beneficiaries of the land.

### **4. Mandatory Sunsetting and Dissolution**
Unlike typical government agencies, NUPALTA-NG is **engineered to disappear**. 
*   **The Sunset Provision:** It must automatically dissolve once all local Community Trust boards have been chartered, seated, and certified by a notary. 
*   **Legacy Transition:** Upon dissolution, oversight is handed over to the independent local boards, with only a single **Legacy Administrator** remaining to handle rare tie-break situations.

### **Operational Funding**
NUPALTA-NG is funded entirely by **private capital** rather than government budgets. Its operations are covered initially by the **Spark Group reservation deposits** and subsequently by a **1% Framework Usage Fee** triggered only upon the successful execution of commercial ground leases.
