Technical note — the 20% compounding mechanism: The 20% annual rent growth is not a projection or assumption. It is mechanically guaranteed by the framework's closed reinvestment loop. Twenty percent of every dollar collected in ground rent is contractually hard-coded back into hub infrastructure — power, roads, maintenance, and Mastery Module skills programs. This continuous infrastructure investment increases the productive capacity and utility value of each hub, which justifies the corresponding rent increase the following year. The tenant's profit margin grows in parallel because the infrastructure they operate on improves every year — they are not paying more for the same asset, they are paying more for a demonstrably better one. The Federal Treasury receives a larger absolute payment each year because the underlying asset is being continuously upgraded at zero cost to the government. The 20% growth rate and the 20% reinvestment fund are the same number because they are the same mechanism: a self-funding, self-appreciating wealth engine with no external fuel source required.
